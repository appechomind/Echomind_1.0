üìû Mystic Call

Effect

In this mind-bending demonstration of telepathic connection, a complete stranger reveals your spectator's innermost thoughts through a random phone call. The spectator thinks of anything - a memory, a dream, a wish - and dials a number they create on the spot. Incredibly, the person who answers knows exactly what they're thinking!

Performance

  1. Ask your spectator to think of something meaningful to them
  2. Have them create a random phone number by rolling dice
  3. They dial the number on their own phone
  4. When someone answers, they experience an impossible moment
  5. The stranger on the line describes their exact thought
  6. End the call and let the mystery linger

Tips

üìû Setting the mood: Create an atmosphere of mystery before they even dial the number.

üìû The thought: Guide them to think of something specific but meaningful - a cherished memory works well.

üìû The aftermath: Don't over-explain what happened. Let them wonder about the mysterious connection they just experienced.
