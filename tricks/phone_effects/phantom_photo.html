Effect

In this eerie demonstration of digital sorcery, a spectator's freely chosen playing card mysteriously appears in a photograph they take with their own phone. What makes this truly inexplicable is that the card appears in a photo of an ordinary deck box, taken moments after the selection - as if the card somehow knew it would be chosen and manifested itself in the image!

Performance

  1. Display a regular deck of cards and have a card freely selected
  2. Place the deck back in its box and hand it to the spectator
  3. Ask them to take a photo of the box with their phone
  4. Have them check the photo they just took
  5. Their chosen card appears to be floating in front of the box!
  6. Let them examine their phone and the deck box - everything is normal

Tips

üì∏ Camera position: Suggest they take the photo straight on, explaining it will create a better composition.

üì∏ Timing is crucial: The moment between the photo being taken and them checking it should feel natural and unhurried.

üì∏ Follow through: Let them take multiple photos if they wish - each one will show the same impossible result.
