üé≠ Mirror Mind

Effect

In this mind-bending demonstration, you'll seemingly establish a telepathic connection with your spectator's phone. They'll think of any emoji, and miraculously, your phone will display the exact same emoji they were thinking of. What makes this even more impossible is that they never tell you their chosen emoji - they simply think of it!

Performance

  1. Ask your spectator to think of any emoji - emphasize that the choice is completely free
  2. Have them open their phone's emoji keyboard but not select anything yet
  3. Tell them to scroll through until they find their chosen emoji
  4. Place your phone face down on the table
  5. Build anticipation by talking about the connection between thoughts and technology
  6. Ask them to tap their chosen emoji when they're ready
  7. Dramatically turn over your phone to reveal the same emoji on your screen

Tips

üé≠ Presentation is key: Don't rush the reveal. Build suspense and make the moment they see the matching emoji as dramatic as possible.

üé≠ Misdirection: While they're searching for their emoji, this is your moment to prepare. Use this time wisely while their attention is on their own phone.

üé≠ Follow-up: If they want to try again, suggest using a different category of emoji to keep things interesting.
